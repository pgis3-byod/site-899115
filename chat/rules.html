Chat Rules

  • Be Respectful – No bullying, harassment, or personal attacks.
  • No Inappropriate Content – No adult content, extreme violence, or offensive imagery.
  • No Hate Speech – Discrimination based on race, gender, sexuality, or similar is not allowed.
  • Keep it Safe – Do not share personal information (real names, addresses, phone numbers, etc.).
  • No Spamming – Do not flood the chat with repeated messages, links, or ads.
  • No Illegal Activity – Do not discuss or promote illegal activities or piracy.
  • Respect Moderators – Follow instructions from moderators and developers.
  • No Impersonation – Do not pretend to be other users or staff.
  • Language Rules – Excessive or abusive swearing may be moderated.
  • Message Control – Developers and moderators can remove any messages at any time without warning.
